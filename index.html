About Wild Festival

Wild Festival is an annual celebration of nature, music, and community. Join us for a weekend filled with exciting workshops, live performances, and unforgettable experiences in the great outdoors.

Workshops

Check out the variety of workshops we have on offer this year. From survival skills to nature photography, there's something for everyone!

Performances

An outstanding variety of performances to see. From folk bands to fire dancers, our lineup is sure to entertain and inspire.
