about

Wild Festival is an annual celebration of nature, music, and community. Join us for a weekend filled with exciting workshops, live performances, and unforgettable experiences in the great outdoors.

Our mission is to reconnect people with nature and foster a sense of environmental stewardship through immersive activities and educational programs.

Whether you're a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or just looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life, Wild Festival has something for everyone. Come and be part of our vibrant community!
